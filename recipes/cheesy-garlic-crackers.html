Cheesy Garlic Crackers Recipe

Home

Description

This recipe was made with the intent to be simple and easy to make, but flavorful at the same time, and it certainly is all of those things. It's especially easy since many people have all of the ingredients and necessary tools to make this.

Ingredients

  • 8 crackers
  • 8 small slices of cheddar cheese
  • Garlic powder

Steps

  1. Lay crackers face-up on a plate.
  2. Put each of your cheese slices on top of the crackers
  3. Put the crackers in the microwave for 10-15 seconds, depending on how powerful your microwave is.
  4. Take the crackers out. The cheese should be melted. Dust the cheesy crackers with garlic powder and serve.
